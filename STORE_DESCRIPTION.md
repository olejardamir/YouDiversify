## YouDiversify — YouTube Music Discovery Tool

Turn YouTube into a music discovery engine. YouDiversify adds a floating player with smart skip controls, voting, channel blocking, and a management dashboard so you can explore new music without repeats.

### How it works

Open any YouTube video, click the YouDiversify toolbar icon, and a floating player appears over the page. From there you can:

- **Skip to fresh content** — automatically avoids videos you've already seen, downvoted, channels you've blocked, and YouTube Mixes
- **Vote and move on** — dislike a video and it skips to the next unvisited recommendation; the downvote is remembered so you never see it again
- **Block channels** — block a channel with one click; YouDiversify silently removes its recommendations from the sidebar
- **Manage your library** — view every video you've visited, sort by title/channel/vote, and build playlists from your history
- **Playlist mode** — filters your visited list by upvoted and neutral videos, with shuffle and repeat, so you can replay what you liked
- **Stay in control** — power toggle, expand/collapse, drag anywhere, light and dark theme

### Perfect for passive listening

Start a video, put it on in the background, and let YouDiversify keep the music moving toward something new. When a track ends, it skips automatically. No repeats, no unwanted channels, no YouTube Mix clutter.

### Privacy

YouDiversify stores all data locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. No accounts, no tracking, no telemetry.

### Permissions explained

- **storage** — saves your visited videos, blocked channels, preferences, and player position locally
- **tabs** — finds your YouTube tab so the floating player can control it
- **scripting** — injects the floating overlay when you click the toolbar icon
- **activeTab** — reads video metadata and controls playback on the current YouTube page
- **Host permissions** — needed so the overlay can appear on any page you open it from
