## YouDiversify — YouTube Music Discovery Tool

Turn YouTube into a music discovery engine. YouDiversify adds a floating player with smart skip controls, voting, channel blocking, and a management dashboard so you can explore new music without repeats.

### How it works

Open any YouTube video, click the YouDiversify toolbar icon, and toggle the floating player over the page. From there you can:

- **Skip to fresh content** — automatically avoids videos you've already seen, downvoted, the current channel, channels you've blocked, and YouTube Mixes
- **Vote and move on** — dislike a video and it skips to the next unvisited different-channel recommendation; the downvote is remembered so it will be skipped automatically unless you open it manually from the manager
- **Block channels** — block a channel with one click; YouDiversify tries one sidebar cleanup action per video using YouTube's own recommendation menu actions
- **Manage your library** — view every video you've visited, sort by title/channel/vote, and build playlists from your history
- **Playlist mode** — filters your visited list by upvoted and neutral videos, with shuffle and repeat, so you can replay what you liked
- **Stay in control** — power toggle, expand/collapse, drag anywhere, light and dark theme

### Perfect for passive listening

Start a video, put it on in the background, and let YouDiversify keep the music moving toward something new. When a track ends, it skips automatically. Automatic skips avoid repeats, unwanted channels, the current channel, and YouTube Mix clutter.

### Privacy

YouDiversify stores all data locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. No accounts, no tracking, no telemetry.

### Permissions explained

- **storage** — saves visited video IDs, titles, URLs, vote state, blocked channel IDs/names, preferences, and player position locally
- **tabs** — finds your YouTube tab so the floating player can control it
- **scripting** — injects the floating overlay when you click the toolbar icon
- **activeTab** — lets the toolbar button inject the floating overlay into the currently active tab
- **Host permissions** — restricted to `https://www.youtube.com/*` for YouTube watch-page control
